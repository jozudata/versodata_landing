Privacy Policy

This is a starter template provided to help activate WhatsApp Business messaging. It is not legal advice — please have it reviewed for your jurisdiction before relying on it for a real business.

Who we are

This bot is operated by the business you are messaging, built and maintained by Versodata Studio. For questions about this policy, contact jozu@versodata.tech.

What we collect

  • Your phone number and name, as provided when booking an appointment.
  • Appointment date, time, and service, as needed to send your reminder.

How we use it

Solely to send appointment reminders and process confirmations or cancellations on behalf of the business you booked with. We do not use your data for advertising.

What we don't do

We don't read or store your personal WhatsApp conversations beyond messages related to your booking. We don't sell your data, and we don't share it beyond the providers strictly needed to operate the service (the messaging platform and the calendar system).

Data retention

We keep your booking information only as long as needed to manage your appointment history with the business.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting the business directly or jozu@versodata.tech.
